Photo Mosaic Generator

Create stunning art from your own photo collection.

Step 1: Upload Tile Photos

Select at least 10-20 small images to be used as the mosaic tiles.

Step 2: Upload Main Photo

Select the main image you want to recreate as a mosaic.

How it Works

This tool uses your browser's Canvas API to analyze the colors of your main photo and replace sections with the small photos you provided. Because it runs locally, your photos are never sent to a server, ensuring 100% privacy.
